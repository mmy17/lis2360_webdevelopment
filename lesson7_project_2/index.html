Who is Billie Eilish?

Billie Eilish, who's full name is Billie Eilish Pirate Baird O'Connell, is an American singer songwriter.She was born in Los Angeles, California on December 18, 2001. She grew up being raised by parents who were in the entertainment industry. Billie was homeschooled her whole life and began writing songs and performing at age 11. She was also a dancer growing up.

Career wise, Billie recorded her first song, titled "Ocean Eyes" in 2015 and released it on the popular music app SoundCloud in 2016. The song became a hit pretty soon after its release and Billie went on to release another song called "Six Feet Under" in the same year. On January 14, 2017, Eilish released an EP with four remixes of "Ocean Eyes". These instantly gained a lot of attention from mainstream audiences which lead her to keep releasing new music.

Following the success of the "Ocean Eyes" remixes, Eilish released her next single "Bellyache" on February 24, 2017. This song was produced and co-written by Finneas O'Connell, her brother who she makes all of her music with. She released a few more notable singles including "Bored" from the 13 Reasons Why soundtrack, Watch, and Copy Cat. Shortly after this she released her critically acclaimed EP entitled "Don't Smile at Me." The commercial success of this EP was unheard of and soon there was great anticipation for a full length Billie Eilish album.

This year in 2019, Eilish finally released her highly anticipated album titled "When We All Fall Asleep Where Do We Go?" The mix of fun dance beats, Eclectic and unique aspects, as well as soft emotional ballods has captivated audiences across the world. Eilish's first album debuted #1 in 17 countries!

Billie Eilish is onlh 17 years old and has already gained so much success and praise. This girl is just getting started she has great things to come in the future!
